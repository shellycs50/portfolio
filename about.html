About•

•Me

I am a driven and curious full-stack developer with flair for abstract problem solving. In my spare time I like to perform music at local pubs, train Thai boxing or relax on a video game. As a child I excelled academically, but it wasn't until taking CS50 my cynicism towards grade oriented teaching was side-lined for an appreciation of useful applied science. I'm excited to expand my problem solving abilities and build cool apps.

CS50 was a trial by fire - hard work, and very rewarding. I spent around 60 hours completing various assignments in C, such as:

  • Writing sorting algorithms
  • Writing 2 election simulators
  • Writing a spellchecker that stores a given dictionary in a hashtable.

Next in CS50 we moved on to Python, SQLite3, HTML CSS and JS, and Flask. I don't remember every bit of syntax from my time in C, but I believe that trial by fire makes grasping context in higher level languages easier.

iO Academy has been very enjoyable. React was rewarding to work in and having learnt to program in C, PHP felt a bit like coming home.

I'm currently studying node via Frontend Masters and Harvard's HSA Dev Bootcamp lectures. Alongside this I'm using Frontend Mentor and Codewars to continue advancing my UI and problem solving skills.
